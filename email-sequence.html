Phase 1+2

Email Sequences - Emily Terrell

Phase 1 + 2 deliverable · Pre-call nurture (3 touches), post-call (4 touches over 14 days), 10-day welcome flow.

Sequence 1 · Welcome Flow (10 days, 5 touches)

Triggered when a new subscriber completes the AI Readiness Scorecard for Real Estate Agents. Segmented by score tier (0-40 / 41-70 / 71-100).

T+0 - Immediate (Score Result)

Subject: Your AI Readiness Score - [X/100] + your action plan inside

Body: Score reveal, one paragraph on what the score means for where the reader is in their AI adoption, three-bullet personalised action plan based on score tier, download link for the PDF action plan, soft CTA to the AI Realtor System course for score tiers 41-70.

Goal: deliver the promise, segment the list, set expectations.

T+1 day - The Workflow Problem

Subject: The task you are probably still doing by hand

Body: A specific workflow story from Emily's experience coaching RE agents on AI adoption. One agent, one problem, one AI solution that took four minutes instead of ninety. No pitch. Closes with "the AI Realtor System module that covers this is Module 2."

Goal: demonstrate depth, connect the course to a real workflow problem the reader recognises.

T+3 days - The Listing Copy Story

Subject: The listing description that got three showing requests in two hours

Body: Specific AI-prompt story tied to a real outcome. Prompt formula teased but not given in full. CTA to the AI Realtor System landing page.

Goal: prove operational depth, route the warm reader to the course.

T+5 days - The Follow-Up Story

Subject: How one agent cleared her follow-up backlog in one afternoon

Body: The personalised follow-up automation story. Specific, not vague. One agent, one workflow, one result. Closes with "Module 3 of the AI Realtor System covers this step-by-step."

Goal: build trust through specificity.

T+7 days - The Scorecard Bridge

Subject: What your score actually means for your next 90 days

Body: Revisit the score tier. What agents at this adoption stage typically do next. The AI Realtor System as the structured path for the 41-70 tier. For the 71-100 tier: a soft route to 1:1 coaching as the next conversation.

Goal: route hot leads to the course or 1:1 application.

T+10 days - Last Touch (segmented)

  • Score 71-100 (1:1 ready): "Your score suggests you are past the course level. The next conversation is a 1:1 strategy session." CTA to Calendly.
  • Score 41-70 (Course pool): Last call for $497 AI Realtor System tier. CTA to course landing.
  • Score 0-40 (Foundation pool): "Start with the free AI tool guide. Come back to the course when the basics are in place." Link to blog post + an invite to re-take the scorecard in 60 days.

Sequence 2 · Post-Event Speaker Follow-Up (5 touches, 14 days)

Triggered when a conference attendee opts in via the speaker deck QR code.

T+0 - Immediate

Subject: Your AI Readiness Score + the action plan from today's talk

Same as welcome flow T+0. Score result + PDF + course CTA for 41-70 tier.

T+2 days - The Tool Recommendation

Subject: The two AI tools I mentioned in the talk - here they are

Specific tool recommendations with context on how to start. No overwhelm. One action item.

T+4 days - The Implementation Story

Subject: What happened when one agent ran module 2 last Monday

One specific implementation story from the course framework. Builds proof that the system works in real RE businesses.

T+7 days - The Course Invite

Subject: The AI Realtor System - for agents who want the full framework

Direct invite to the $497 course tier. Clear what is included, clear what the outcome is. No over-sell.

T+14 days - The 1:1 Option

Subject: If the course feels like too much right now

For agents who have not yet purchased the course, this email opens the 1:1 coaching conversation as an alternative entry point. Clean ask, no pressure.

Sequence 3 · Pre-Call Nurture (3 touches before 1:1 discovery call)

Triggered when a prospect books a 1:1 coaching discovery call.

T-7 days

Subject: Before our call - three things I will ask you

The three questions Emily will ask on the call. Helps the prospect arrive prepared and positions the call as a working session, not a pitch.

T-3 days

Subject: What I learned from coaching RE teams on AI this year

One story-driven insight from Emily's real coaching experience. Sets the room. Shows what kind of thinking happens in Emily's coaching container.

T-1 day

Subject: Tomorrow at [time] - one thing to bring

Calendar confirmation, one specific prep ask (current CRM or follow-up workflow description, transaction volume, one workflow they most want to fix).

Sequence 4 · Post-Call Follow-Up (4 touches over 14 days)

Triggered after the 1:1 discovery call regardless of outcome.

T+1 hour - Thank You + Recap

Subject: Recap from our call + next steps

One paragraph recap, two specific action items from the call, link to the agreed-upon next step (course enrolment, 1:1 coaching agreement, or waiting list).

T+3 days - The Specific Resource

Subject: The [resource] I mentioned on the call

Whatever Emily committed to sending during the call. Delivered clean - PDF, shared Notion doc, or blog post link.

T+7 days - The Check-In

Subject: Quick - did you try [X] yet?

Soft accountability check. "Reply with a one-line update if you did. Reply with a question if you got stuck."

T+14 days - The Decision

Subject: Where you landed

Clean ask: "Are we moving forward with [agreed next step] or are you holding off for now?" No pressure. Clears the pipeline in either direction.

Subject Line Bank (rotation)

For ongoing broadcasts beyond the welcome and nurture sequences:

  • "The AI prompt I use before every listing appointment"
  • "Three agents, three workflows, three hours saved per week"
  • "What I would do differently if I were still selling RE right now"
  • "The follow-up system that runs without me"
  • "Why most agents try AI once and give up"
  • "The CMA that took me seven minutes to write"
  • "AI for real estate - the part no one talks about at the conference"

Triggers + Tags

  • Tag by score tier on quiz completion
  • Tag by purchase (AI Realtor System $497 / $997 / 1:1 coaching inquiry)
  • Tag by entry point (organic IG / blog / speaker event QR)
  • Tag by content engagement (opened 5+ in 30 days = "warm agent")
  • Auto-suppress promotional during launch weeks (replace with launch-specific track)
  • Re-engagement campaign at 60 days of inactivity
