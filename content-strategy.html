Premise

Speaker Content Pivot Brief

Current state: Emily speaks at conferences and produces blog content + IG posts independently. No through-line between conference appearances and the owned content channels. Post-event traffic from speaking engagements routes nowhere specific.

Phase 3 move: Launch a "From The Stage" content series on IG. Each conference talk generates one 10-15 minute "After The Talk" YouTube video (the same framework, extended with implementation detail Emily did not have time for on stage) plus 6-8 short-form cuts for IG reels and YouTube shorts. The QR code on the speaker deck routes to the AI Readiness Scorecard. The YouTube video routes to the scorecard via the description link.

Why: Zero new content creation pressure beyond what she is already doing on stage. Extends conference IP into an owned content asset with a built-in conversion event.

Photoshoot Brief

One-day shoot, mid-cycle in Phase 3.

Morning - Professional Setting: Emily at desk or clean workspace, laptop open, clean RE or coaching aesthetic. Natural light preferred. 100+ stills. B-roll of her working at the desk, reviewing content, typing.

Afternoon - Speaker / Educator Angle: Emily at a whiteboard or in front of a clean backdrop that suggests a presentation context. Relaxed, confident, collegial register. 80+ stills. 1-2 hours of talking-head B-roll for IG reels and course asset.

Outputs feed: AI Realtor System landing page, scorecard result page, IG content calendar, speaker bio page, hub page.

Short-Form Roadmap (90 days)

  • Weeks 1-4: post 4 hooks per week from Categories 1-3 (AI adoption, listing copy, productivity) - highest activation triggers for working agents
  • Weeks 5-8: post 4 per week from Categories 4-6 (coaching, market positioning, speaker angle)
  • Weeks 9-12: post 4 per week from Categories 7-8 (implementation, personal) + retargeting ads layered on organic

Cadence: 4 IG reels + 4 blog posts repurposed into carousels + 1 long-form YouTube per week. Repurpose ratio 1:4 - one filmed or written asset becomes four distribution formats.

Conversion Goal

Every piece of content has the same final CTA: "Take the AI Readiness Scorecard - 4 minutes, custom action plan, in your inbox" with the link in bio routing to the scorecard quiz.
