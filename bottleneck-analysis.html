Three Bottlenecks I Found Inside Your Funnel - Emily Terrell

All three install in less than 7 days from kickoff. Built to compound. Order by impact.

Snapshot

  • 7.6K IG @coachemilyterrell + 1,418 posts + active blog at coachemilyterrell.com
  • Top AI-for-RE speaker positioning at sub-10K audience
  • Current offer routes only to 1:1 coaching or contact form
  • No productised AI-realtor system for sale
  • No lead magnet, no quiz, no scorecard, no email-nurture infrastructure
  • Speaker authority not captured in any evergreen revenue layer

Bottleneck #1 - Speaker Authority Routes to 1:1 Only, Leaving the Entire Mid-Tier Unbuilt

The asymmetry: Emily is recognised as a top AI-for-RE speaker. Every conference talk, every blog post, every Instagram reel builds the authority. Agents who see her speak want to implement what she teaches. Right now, the only conversion path is booking a 1:1 coaching call - the highest-friction, lowest-volume option. There is no productised AI Realtor System sitting between "watched Emily's talk" and "can afford 1:1 coaching."

A $497-$997 "AI Realtor System" - course + prompt library + implementation templates + AI tool scorecard - gives every conference attendee and IG follower a place to spend money at a price that converts, without competing with 1:1 coaching. It becomes the feeder tier that qualifies and warms the 1:1 buyer pool.

Why it is costing her: - Conference attendees hear the talk, want to act, and have nowhere to spend $500-$997 with Emily today - The blog content and IG posts are building demand for a system that does not exist yet - Every week the AI-for-RE category grows, a competitor with a productised offer captures the buyers Emily is educating

The fix: - "AI Realtor System" - 5-module self-paced course - Modules: AI tool selection for RE, prompt engineering for listings + outreach, lead nurturing automation, AI-powered CMA + market reports, implementation sprint and accountability - Includes a prompt library and templates bundle - $497 standard, $997 with a personal AI-readiness audit of the buyer's RE business from Emily

Estimated impact: Year-1 floor $100-250K/yr at 200-500 buyers across the speaker circuit and IG audience.

Why this is #1: Highest ceiling. The speaker positioning is already building demand. A productised system captures the demand she is currently creating for competitors who have one.

Bottleneck #2 - 7.6K Followers and 1,418 Posts With No Email Capture Event

The leak: The @coachemilyterrell content engine is producing daily posts, reels, and blog content. Agents are watching, reading, and following. There is no lead magnet, no quiz, and no scorecard between "consumes Emily's content" and "books a 1:1 call." Most followers never book the call. They stay warm on social and then drift to wherever the next AI-for-RE voice sends them.

An "AI Readiness Scorecard for Real Estate Agents" - 8 questions, 4 minutes, returns a score 0-100 with a personalised action plan - captures email, segments by AI adoption stage, and starts a nurture sequence that routes warm leads to the AI Realtor System course or 1:1 coaching based on their score.

Why it is costing her: - 7.6K followers with no email list means zero owned audience - Without segmentation, Emily cannot tell which followers are ready to buy a $497 course versus who needs 1:1 coaching versus who is not yet in the buying window - The blog audience and IG audience are rented, not owned. The email list is the asset.

The fix: - "AI Readiness Scorecard for Real Estate Agents" - quiz on ScoreApp or Typeform - 8 questions: current AI tools used, adoption stage, biggest workflow bottleneck, transaction volume - Returns personalised score + PDF action plan + email nurture sequence segmented by tier - Bio-link router updated so the scorecard is the primary conversion event

Estimated impact: Year-1 lift $30-80K/yr incremental from email-driven course and 1:1 coaching fills. Compounds for life of the list.

Why this is #2: Foundational. The email list is the asset every other bottleneck rides on. Fast install in less than 7 days. Compounds infinitely once live.

Bottleneck #3 - Speaker Follow-Up Infrastructure Does Not Exist

The gap: Emily speaks at conferences on AI for real estate. She is in the room with 50-500 RE professionals who are primed, attentive, and motivated. Then the talk ends. There is no QR code to a scorecard. No post-event email sequence. No follow-up asset that keeps the conference audience warm for 30-90 days after the talk. The conference moment is the highest-intent touchpoint in the funnel and it is not being captured.

A speaker follow-up system - QR code on every slide deck, instant scorecard opt-in at the talk, automated 5-touch post-event nurture sequence, soft pitch to the AI Realtor System at touch 3 - keeps every conference audience warm and routes the warmest leads into the course or a 1:1 discovery call.

Why it is costing her: - Conference speaking is time-intensive and produces zero owned assets if there is no follow-up capture - RE agents attending an AI-for-RE talk are in the highest-intent buying window for an AI Realtor System - Every conference Emily speaks at without a follow-up system is a batch of warmed buyers she loses to decay

The fix: - Speaker QR slide (included in every deck): routes to AI Readiness Scorecard - Immediate opt-in confirmation email with score + action plan - 5-touch post-event nurture: Day 0 (score delivery), Day 2 (tool recommendation), Day 4 (implementation story), Day 7 (AI Realtor System invite), Day 14 (last call + 1:1 coaching option) - Speaker bio page on coachemilyterrell.com updated to include scorecard opt-in and course pre-sell

Estimated impact: Year-1 floor $20-50K additional from conference follow-up captures that currently go cold. Compounds with every additional speaking engagement added.

Why this is #3: Smaller ceiling than #1, dependent on Bottleneck #2 for nurture infrastructure, but it directly extends the ROI of time already being spent on speaking. The highest-intent buyers in any funnel are in the room at a live talk.
