VSL Preview Script - Emily Terrell

Phase 1 deliverable · Script structure, page layout, production notes for the AI Realtor System hero VSL.

Format

8-12 minute conversational VSL hosted on Wistia, embedded in the landing-page hero. Optional 3-4 minute "Quick Intro" version for paid-traffic top-of-funnel. Single take where possible, supplemented by B-roll of Emily working at a laptop, looking at AI tools on screen, and in a clean workspace or coaching context.

Cold Open (00:00 - 00:45)

On camera, Emily:

"Two years ago I was spending three hours every Tuesday writing listing descriptions, follow-up emails, and market reports that I knew were important but that I also knew I was going to be doing again next Tuesday. Same three hours, same tasks, same sense that I was working in my business instead of on it. Then I started testing AI tools inside my actual real estate workflow. Not the demos. Not the tutorials. Inside live transactions, real follow-up sequences, actual listing appointments. What I built over those two years is what this course is."

The Specific Problem (00:45 - 03:00)

What the average working RE agent is dealing with right now:

  • Still writing listing descriptions from scratch every time
  • Follow-up is manual, inconsistent, and always the first thing to get skipped when the week gets busy
  • CMA prep takes 90 minutes of work that could take 12 minutes with the right prompt
  • Market reports feel like a chore to write and produce because they are
  • AI tools downloaded and abandoned because nobody showed how they work inside a real transaction

Specific friction numbers: a RE agent handling 20 transactions per year spends an estimated 200+ hours on written communication tasks that AI can handle in under 40. The AI Realtor System finds those 160 hours and routes them back to the work that requires a human.

Who I Am (03:00 - 05:00)

Direct, not chronological:

  • Real estate business coach working with agents at all stages of their practice
  • Two years of live testing: AI tools inside real estate transactions, follow-up workflows, listing copy, and market reporting
  • Speaker on AI adoption in real estate (specific conferences: Emily names her own events)
  • Built the AI Realtor System because agents who came to my talks kept asking for the implementation version, not just the overview

The point is not the credentials. The point is the two years of live testing inside real transactions.

What the Course Is Built On (05:00 - 08:00)

Walk through the five modules:

  1. AI Tool Selection for Real Estate - the stack that works for RE workflows (not the generic tools, the ones tested inside actual transactions)
  2. Prompt Engineering for Listings and Outreach - the specific prompt formulas for listing descriptions, buyer emails, follow-up sequences, and objection responses
  3. Lead Nurturing Automation with AI - how to build a follow-up system that personalises at scale without a VA
  4. AI-Powered CMA and Market Reports - the research and writing workflow that cuts prep time from 90 minutes to under 15
  5. Implementation Sprint - a structured 5-day process to get every module running inside your RE business before the sprint ends

Each module gets one real example, one prompt or system, one outcome. Not theory. Not demos. Implementation.

Who This Is For - And Who It Is Not (08:00 - 09:30)

This is for: working RE agents who want AI running inside their real workflow, not just in concept. Coaches who want to implement before recommending. Agents who have tried AI tools and given up because nobody showed them the RE-specific prompts.

This is not for: agents who have zero transactions and want AI to fix that (AI is an efficiency tool, not a lead generation engine on its own). Agents looking for automation magic without any implementation work. Complete beginners who have never used a CRM or follow-up tool (the Foundation blog posts come first).

What Is Included and the Investment (09:30 - 11:00)

  • Five modules, self-paced, lifetime access
  • Full prompt library: 50+ prompts organised by RE workflow category (listing copy, buyer outreach, CMA, market reports, follow-up sequences)
  • Templates bundle: listing description templates, follow-up sequence templates, CMA format templates
  • 5-day implementation sprint guide
  • $497 standard tier, $997 for the AI Readiness Audit tier (Emily reviews the buyer's current RE workflow and identifies the three highest-ROI AI integrations for their specific practice)

Close (11:00 - 12:00)

"The agents who are going to look back in two years and be glad they started are starting now. The course is below this video. The implementation sprint starts on day one of enrolment. - Emily."

Production Notes

  • Filming location: Clean workspace setup (desk, laptop, minimal background) or neutral coaching context. Emily does not need a professional studio - natural light and a quality mic are enough.
  • Camera: iPhone 14 Pro or higher with a Rode Wireless Go clip mic is sufficient for the course VSL. No need for a full crew for the first version.
  • Wardrobe: professional but approachable. Business casual. Real estate professional aesthetic without being stiff.
  • Pace: conversational. Emily should speak at her natural coaching pace. Cuts are fine at natural pauses.
  • Music: none under talking head. Optional light ambient bed under B-roll of the laptop screen.
  • Captions: SRT for Wistia. Burnt-in for any short-form cuts used on IG.
  • Thumbnail (Wistia poster): Emily at desk looking at camera, clean and professional, laptop open. Title overlay: "The AI Realtor System."

Short Cuts (for paid traffic + IG)

From the long-form, edit:

  • 1 x 3-4 min Quick Intro (cold ad asset + YouTube)
  • 3 x 60-90 sec hook-led trailers pulled from the cold open and module walk-through
  • 5 x 30-45 sec module-specific cuts (one per module)
  • 5 x 15-20 sec IG reel hooks pulled from the specific-problem section

Distribution

  • Long-form Wistia: landing-page hero
  • 3-4 min Quick Intro: YouTube + cold paid traffic
  • 60-90 sec trailers: IG reels + YouTube shorts (top of funnel)
  • 30-45 sec module cuts: IG content calendar (Phase 3)
